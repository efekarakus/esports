Insights

- Retention

The number of viewers whenever a game is played is strictly increasing during every game played in this tournament! This most likely suggests that games have a high retention rate. Viewers don't leave a game after starting to watch it. They tend to stick through the whole show. Furthermore, more viewers join the broadcast as the game is played.
